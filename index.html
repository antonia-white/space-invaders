Mayday.

The intergalactic federation has identified that your vessle is about to fall seige to a swarm of hostile lifeforms. Your only hope for survival is to shoot your way out. This warning should give those of you onboard some short notice to prepare.

To maneuver your ship on keyboard use A and D keys or left and right arrow keys. Alternatively you may drag the ship with your cursor or finger if using a touch screen device.

To shoot the hostiles press SPACE on keyboard or left click on mouse. Simply tap the game area to shoot on touchscreen devices.

BE WARNED: maneuvering by dragging the spaceship into position currently results in a less heroic (aka easier) gameplay.

We at the intergalactic federation are dismayed to relay this information to you. The situation looks bleak. May the light of the nine shine on you all.

We pray for you and your crews safe passage.


END COMMS.
